Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 22, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Spacescape! We are committed to protecting your privacy and personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the Spacescape mobile game application.

2. Information We Collect

Spacescape is designed as an offline game that does not require an internet connection. We DO NOT collect the following types of information:

  • Personal information (name, email address, phone number)
  • Location data or GPS coordinates
  • Contact information or address book
  • Online usage data or analytics
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Photos, camera, or microphone access
  • Social media account information

The application only stores locally on your device:

  • High scores and game achievements
  • Game settings (sound preferences, control settings)
  • Spaceship unlock progress
  • User preferences and customizations

3. How We Use Information

All data is stored locally on your device and is used exclusively to:

  • Save your game progress and achievements
  • Maintain your personal game settings
  • Display high scores and statistics
  • Provide a personalized gaming experience
  • Remember your spaceship selections and unlocks

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We DO NOT share, sell, rent, or disclose any of your information to third parties. Since the application operates completely offline, no data is transmitted over the internet or to external servers.

We do not engage in:

  • Data mining or profiling
  • Advertising or marketing data collection
  • Third-party analytics or tracking
  • Cross-app data sharing

5. Data Security

Your data is protected through:

  • Local storage on your device only
  • No network transmission of personal data
  • Access restricted to the Spacescape application only
  • Standard mobile operating system security measures
  • No cloud storage or remote backups

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Delete all game data by uninstalling the application
  • Reset game progress through in-app settings
  • Modify sound and control preferences at any time
  • Clear high scores and achievements
  • Customize game settings without data collection

7. Children's Privacy

Spacescape is suitable for all ages and is designed to be family-friendly. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age. Since our application does not collect any personal data, there are no privacy risks for children using our game.

Parents can feel confident that:

  • No personal information is collected from children
  • No online interactions or chat features exist
  • No in-app purchases or external links are present
  • The game operates completely offline

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal and regulatory reasons. Any changes will be communicated through app updates with a new effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

9. Third-Party Services

Spacescape does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks. The game operates independently without external dependencies or data sharing.
